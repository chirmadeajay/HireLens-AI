# 2-Minute Demo Video Script

Hi everyone, this is my Weekly AI Generalist Hackathon project called HireLens AI.

The real-world problem I am solving is resume screening. Recruiters and hiring managers often spend 2 to 5 hours reading resumes, comparing them with a job description, identifying skill gaps, and preparing interview questions.

HireLens AI compresses that workflow into a few minutes.

Here is how it works.

First, I paste the job description into the app.

Next, I upload multiple resumes in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format.

Now I click Analyze Candidates.

The app uses AI to read every resume, compare it against the job description, and generate structured hiring insights.

Here you can see the ranked shortlist. Each candidate gets a fit score, a decision like Strong Match or Possible Match, and recommended role fit.

Now I open a candidate deep dive. The app shows why this candidate is a good fit, what areas are missing or weak, interview questions to ask, and risk flags for the recruiter.

The user can also download a shortlist CSV and a full screening report.

This is useful because it saves recruiter time, reduces repetitive manual work, and makes first-round screening more consistent.

The project uses free tools: Python, Streamlit, Groq API, and open-source document parsing libraries. It can be deployed for free on Streamlit Community Cloud.

Thank you.
